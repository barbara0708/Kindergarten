Our Classes & Activities

Little Chef Cooking School

Little Chef Cooking School, founded in 2007, offers hands-on cooking classes to kids and families. The class' mission is to “open your eyes to the joy of cooking”, and to have fun while learning new skills. Kids pick their own menu at Cooking Birthday Parties (also in English, French, Italian, and Russian) and discover culinary Warsaw at Cooking Day Camp (summer and winter). Young chefs also have fun and learn cooking at Camps by the Baltic Sea and sail-and-cook at Little Chef Globtroter Sailing Camp in Mazury.

Hiking Green Trees Class

Hiking Green Trees Class offers walks in the woods near the kindergarten building. Going on a hike is a great way to encourage kids to be active and connect with nature A light hike offers beneficial cardiovascular exercise, for sure, but perhaps more important in today's world of highly groomed playspaces, a trail hike can offer kids opportunities to traverse rocks, navigate exposed roots, and climb over fallen trees, building balance and agility.

Music class

We teach an extremely broad range of instruments and musical genres, in one-to-one or small group music lessons. Music World, located in Wilanów, offers music classes to children and adults – singing, piano and keyboard lessons, music and rhythm classes. Lessons at Midori Music Studio include individual and group vocal lessons, preparation for singing competitions and “talent shows”, individual piano lessons, as well as group workshops – music and movement workshops with music from all over the world.

English Lessons

Our teachers and staff members prioritise the safety and happiness of our students. The team is experienced in moving children from place to place and in engaging with them whilst in transit. We use the time on the Tube or bus as an opportunity to speak and interact with the children, drawing out the shy ones from their shells. Our experienced, native English-speaking teachers, teaching assistants and workshop leaders encourage the children to 'have a go' at expressing their thoughts in English as much as possible.

Gymnastics

We provide developmentally appropriate gym activities to enhance each child’s growth. Our center’s state-of-the-art gym facilities are child friendly and safe. Well trained instructors introduce children to the basics of gym class in creative and fun ways. As they become comfortable learning new things and performing in front of adults and other children, they develop a sense of self-confidence.

Drawing Class

In this class, your child will be introduced to basic art techniques. Designed to give your child the chance to explore a wide range of materials and media, the class covers model making, printmaking, puppetry, painting and drawing. Children will explore the great world are art using oil pastels, clay, paper and paint. This unique learning experience places an emphasis on having fun while exploring creativity!

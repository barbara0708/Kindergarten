The Little Explorers Way
Our philosophy

At Little Explorers, our priority is the happiness of our pupils. We want every child to feel safe and secure and endeavour to create a caring, supportive environment that enables each individual to develop and shine.

What is important for us
Our values and goals

With the help of teachers and the environment as the “third teacher,” students have opportunities to confidently take risks in all areas of their development. No matter their individual learning syle and unique contributions, students know that they are integral, important members of their class and school community.

And lastly, this one.
Our community.

With passion and enthusiasm, our talented early-childhood educators set children down the path toward becoming lifelong learners, equipped with the skills they need to take on challenges in their future and the become change-makers of tomorrow.
